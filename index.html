About Us

Eurolux Australia is a family owned business, established in Australia in 1999.

We are committed to sourcing and supplying only the most superior home and garden products and to providing the highest level of customer service. All our products are of the highest quality and are considered among the best of their kind. Each product has been chosen for its quality, durability and reliability. Our product range includes Eurolux Cookware: German made, non-stick, suitable from stove-top to oven cookware along with a growing number of innovative home and gardening tools of the highest quality. All our products are proudly manufactured in Europe or Australia.

The Sure Load was added to our range only a few years ago and has been designed by Eurolux Australia, and is manufactured in Australia. It was developed in response to the enormous frustration often faced by gardeners, with fiddly and complicated line changes to their existing trimmer heads. The unique Sure Load system fits all petrol driven trimmers and requires no effort to reload.

We retail our products online, and also maintain close contact with our customers by attending the major Home and Garden shows, Field days and Trade Events. We also have a growing number or stockists throughout Australia.

We look forward to sharing our wonderful range with you and welcome your comments and feedback.
